About

Here is a simple introduction about me...

Hey there, I'm Yvonne De Guzman and is a Junior Full Stack Web Developer who recently completed an internship at KodeGo. I has a portfolio with sample work and loves creating awesome, user-friendly web designs.
I have picked up many skills along the way, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, PHP, Node.js, API's, MySQL, and Laravel. I am proficient in creating beautiful front-end interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am familiar with frameworks such as Bootstrap and React. I am also proficient in back-end development with PHP, Node.js, and Laravel, and I am also familiar with databases such as MySQL.
It's not just about the code for me. I'm all about creating visually appealing and user-friendly web applications. I've got a keen eye for detail and a knack for integrating APIs to add that extra touch of interactivity to my projects.
I am a hardworking individual who is always eager to learn new skills and take on new challenges. I am a team player, dedicated to my work, and committed to meeting deadlines. I have a strong portfolio of work that reflects my skills and expertise. I am excited to join the team and contribute to the success of your web development projects. Let's work together to create some amazing outcomes.
